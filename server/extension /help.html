How to use ?

Yaarthartha uses artificial intelligence to classify the fake and real news using various classifiers .

Steps in using it

  • First open the extension , initially it automatically fetches news and below the detected news classified news is shown
  • You can report news through report link option
  • You can also detect news directly through a link
  • Green : Real , Red: Fake
